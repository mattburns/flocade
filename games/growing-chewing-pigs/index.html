GROWING,
CHEWING, PIGS

  1. 🌱 Pick a seed and tap a soil patch to plant apples, oranges, cabbages or carrots.
  2. 🚿 Water every plant, every day — thirsty plants wilt and die!
  3. 🌙 Press the moon to switch to night, then back to day. Each morning your watered plants grow.
  4. ✋ When they're fully grown, use the hand to pick fruit or pull carrots, then drag the food and drop it in the pig's mouth. Chomp chomp!
  5. 🐷 Feeding keeps your pig happy. A hungry pig is a sad pig…
  6. 📸 Press the camera any time to save a snapshot to your gallery.
